## Production Notes

### Visual Style

- **Theme:** Light mode throughout for clarity
- **Speed:** 1.2-1.5x speed for workout session parts
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts (0.2s), no fancy transitions needed
- **Fingers:** Show natural hand/finger taps for authenticity

### Text Overlays

- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif (Montserrat or Inter)
- **Size:** Large and readable on mobile (48-60px)
- **Position:** Bottom third or top third, never center
- **Style:** White text with dark shadow/outline
- **Animation:** Fade in + slight slide up (0.3s duration)

### Audio (Optional but Recommended)

- **Music:** Upbeat, energetic instrumental (120-130 BPM)
- **Sound effects:**
  - Tap sounds when buttons pressed
  - Whoosh for transitions
  - Success chime at workout complete
- **Volume:** Keep low if adding music (85% watch muted)

### Captions/Subtitles

- All text overlays should be self-explanatory
- Use emojis sparingly in text overlays for visual interest (üí™, ‚úÖ, üî•)

## Technical Requirements

### Device Preparation

1. **Clean the app:**
   - No debug info visible
   - No "Test User" placeholder text
   - Battery full (or hide status bar)
   - Good WiFi signal (or hide)
   - Time showing ~10:30 AM (looks professional)

2. **Screen recording settings:**
   - **iOS:** Built-in screen recorder at 60fps
   - **Android:** Built-in or AZ Screen Recorder
   - **Resolution:** Native device resolution (1080x1920 or higher)
   - **Frame rate:** 60fps for smooth motion

### Recording Tips

1. **Practice the flow 3-4 times** before recording
2. **Record each scene separately** (easier to edit)
3. **Do multiple takes** - pick the smoothest one
4. **Keep movements natural** - don't rush taps
5. **Hold on important screens** for 1-2 seconds before transitions

### Post-Production Checklist

- [ ] Trim to exactly 20-25 seconds
- [ ] Speed up workout session to 1.2-1.5x
- [ ] Add text overlays with animations
- [ ] Add subtle transitions between scenes
- [ ] Color grade for consistency (bump saturation +10%)
- [ ] Add background music (optional)
- [ ] Export at 1080x1920, 60fps, high bitrate
